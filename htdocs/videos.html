How to use a BreadBoard - Electronics Basics 10

How to connect an LED and resistor to a breadboard to create a simple circuit. The LED is a polarized component, viewers need to make sure that the positive leg is connected to the positive side of the power supply and the negative leg is connected to the negative side of the power supply. The resistor is used to limit the current flow to the LED and prevent it from burning out.
